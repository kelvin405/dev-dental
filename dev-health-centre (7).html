Combined General & Dental Care

Modern treatment · Experienced doctors · Walk-ins welcome

"Dev Health Centre is a combined General Practice & Dental Clinic offering quality care under one roof."

Our general clinic treats common health issues like fever, cough, cold, diarrhoea and body pain, while our dental wing provides care for gum diseases, cavities, tooth pain, and more. We are open daily from 5:30 PM to 9 PM, except Sundays and government holidays.
